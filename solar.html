Solar


There are two major types of solar panels: photovoltaic and solar thermal collectors. Photovoltaic solar panels use particles from the sun to turn into electricity. Solar thermal collectors use heat from the sun to warm liquid within a network of pipes. Solar panels are becoming more and more popular. There are even $0 down solar loan options. Find out more about these as well as the hows and whys of solar panels here.
